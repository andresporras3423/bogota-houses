About us

BOGOTA HOUSES was created with the purpose of offering a platform where homeowners and sellers in Bogotá can post their properties to sale or rent. We avoid charging monthly fees and try to keep our business model as simple as possible. Everyone can post their property for free here and only pay if they want it marked as urgent or premium. The urgent and premium flags last forever and there is no need to renew them.
